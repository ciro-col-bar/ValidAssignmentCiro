INT. DETECTIVE'S OFFICE - NIGHT

Rain hammers the window. DETECTIVE SARAH COLE, 40s, sits at a cluttered desk, studying a photograph under a single lamp. She exhales slowly, sets the photo down.

EXT. ROOFTOP - CONTINUOUS

The city sprawls below. Cole steps out onto the rain-soaked rooftop, collar turned up. She scans the skyline with a pair of binoculars. A distant figure moves in a lit window.

INT. ABANDONED WAREHOUSE - LATER

Cole pushes through a rusted door. Flashlight beam cuts through dust. Rows of covered crates stretch into darkness. She moves carefully, hand on her holster.
